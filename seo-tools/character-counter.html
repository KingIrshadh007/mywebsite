What is a Character Counter?

A Character Counter is a text analysis tool that calculates the number of characters, words, lines and characters excluding spaces in your content.

Why Use a Character Counter?

  • Meet social media character limits.
  • Optimize meta descriptions and titles.
  • Analyze content length instantly.
  • Improve SEO writing.
  • Track words and line counts easily.

How to Use

  • Type or paste your content.
  • The result updates automatically.
  • View characters, words and lines instantly.
  • Use the counts for SEO or content writing.

Who Can Use It?

  • Students
  • Bloggers
  • SEO Professionals
  • Content Writers
  • Researchers
  • Social Media Managers
