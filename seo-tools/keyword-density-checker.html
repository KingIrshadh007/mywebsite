What is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a keyword appears compared to the total number of words in a piece of content.

Why is Keyword Density Important?

  • Helps optimize content for SEO.
  • Prevents keyword stuffing.
  • Improves content relevance.
  • Enhances search engine visibility.
  • Supports better ranking opportunities.

How to Use

  • Paste your content.
  • Enter the keyword to analyze.
  • Click "Check Density".
  • View keyword count and density percentage.
  • Optimize your content accordingly.
