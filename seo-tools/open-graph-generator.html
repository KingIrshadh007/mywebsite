📱 Open Graph Generator

Generate Open Graph meta tags for social media sharing.

What are Open Graph Tags?

Open Graph tags are meta tags used by Facebook, WhatsApp, LinkedIn and other social platforms to display your webpage with a title, description and image when shared.

Benefits

  • Improve social media appearance.
  • Increase click-through rates.
  • Display custom images and descriptions.
  • Enhance brand visibility.
  • Work with Facebook, LinkedIn and WhatsApp.

How to Use

  • Enter title and description.
  • Add page URL.
  • Enter image URL.
  • Click Generate Open Graph Tags.
  • Copy and paste into your website head section.
