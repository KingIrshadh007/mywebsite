🌐 URL Encoder & Decoder

Encode URLs for safe transmission or decode encoded URLs back to readable format.

What is URL Encoding?

URL encoding converts special characters and spaces into a format that can be safely transmitted through web addresses and APIs.

Example

Input: 

https://example.com/search?q=hello world

Encoded Output: 

https%3A%2F%2Fexample.com%2Fsearch%3Fq%3Dhello%20world

Features

  • Encode URLs safely.
  • Decode encoded URLs instantly.
  • Browser-based processing.
  • Fast and secure.
  • No data leaves your device.

How to Use

  • Click Encode or Decode.
  • Copy the output.
  • Clear everything with one click.
