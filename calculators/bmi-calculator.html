What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: Below 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 - 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 - 29.9
  • Obese: 30 and above
