What is PPF?

PPF (Public Provident Fund) is a long-term government-backed savings scheme offering tax benefits and guaranteed returns.

Benefits of PPF

  • Tax-free returns
  • Government-backed security
  • Long-term wealth creation
  • Suitable for retirement planning
