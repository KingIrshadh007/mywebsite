What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly into mutual funds and build wealth through compounding.

Benefits of SIP

  • Disciplined investing
  • Power of compounding
  • Affordable monthly investments
  • Long-term wealth creation
